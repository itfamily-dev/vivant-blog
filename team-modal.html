Michael is a Silicon Valley innovator and entrepreneur who has been seduced by the world of wine. He has started and built several successful technology companies, including big data pioneer, Splunk (Nasdaq: SPLK). His foundation, FOUNDER.org, has invested in more than 125 young innovators applying technology to some of the world’s most challenging problems.

Michael and his family are the fifth family to steward the storybook Château de Pommard, in Burgundy, and have converted the property into a beacon of biodynamic viticulture.

He graduated from Drexel University with a Bachelor of Science in Computer Science and Mathematics where he is recognized as a Drexel 100 top living alumni. Michael also holds an MBA in Marketing and Finance from The Wharton School at the University of Pennsylvania, and he is a certified Wine & Spirit Education Trust wine professional.
