What is a Private Event?

Private Events allow you to organize and attend a Live Experience held just for you and your guests. From team-building with your group, celebrating sales milestones, or a premium gathering for your best clients, VIVANT Private Events will connect you like never before. We take care of every detail. We’ll help you choose the Experience, deliver the tasting kits to each guest and send the invitations. Tell us about your event, and we’ll do the rest.
